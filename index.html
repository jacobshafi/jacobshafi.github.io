About Me

Projects

NewCo

While working on InstaApp, my team and I developed NewCo's web platform. NewCo orchestrates festivals, hosting various businesses and organizations with the goal of educating on company culture and connecting people to industry. Users of the web app can explore different companies and connect with other users, developing a personal and professional network.

I worked on the backend and data infrastructure and facilitated that data to the front end using Ruby on Rails and JavaScript. Additionally, I developed the Admin Dashboard, giving admins the ability to send batch emails to previous users and create new festivals. I also worked on the frontend for NewCo's website using HTML and CSS.

InstaApp

In the Fall of 2015, I started InstaApp with three other engineers. InstaApp is a freelance contracting company that helps turn clients' ideas into fully functional and scalable apps. I participated in building both the front and back-end functionalities for each project and consulted with clients about progress and future developments.

Tacho

Tacho is an app that connects lawyers to people seeking legal consultation. I designed a web-scraping algorithm that collected data on all lawyers in the state of California. From the scraped data, I designed the infrastructure necessary to create lawyer profiles. I also designed a live chat room that allowed users to communicate with lawyers one on one.

FabExchange

I interned at FabExchange for 3 months in the summer of 2014. During this time, I designed and tested front-end components of website using HTML and CSS.
